Featured Testimonial

I moved from Maine to Florida in the middle of my orthodontic Treatments and I had to scramble to find an orthodontic to tighten my braces. Dr. Feldman is excellent. He explained everything to me and was always available to listen when I had questions. His staff is friendly and never seems to be put out if i have to reschedule my visit. My schedule visits are always within the acceptable time frame for me (if my apt is 9:30, I am seen by 9:35 at the latest). His office is clean and bright. But what I enjoy most is how Dr. Feldman supports the community. He sponsors events and does fundraisers in order to support the local community. He is a great Dr. I will and have recommended him to everyone.

— Amanda P., Tampa, FL (Review of us on Yelp )
